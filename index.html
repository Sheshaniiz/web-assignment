 CASUAL WOMEN 

If you want to stay trendy and stylish always, you cannot compromise on the casual style. You must have great casual wear in your wardrobe that you can wear on a daily basis. Casual style first appeared in the beginning of the 20th century in the USA. People started shifting from uncomfortable, glittery clothes to jeans, T-shirts, and cowboy boots. Gradually, changes became prominent in casual wear for women too. Casual wear for women is all about comfortable and relaxed clothes that can help you make a style statement. For example, sometimes you can look fashionable in simple T-shirt, jeans, and sandals. There are interesting and easy to handle options in casual wear gowns too. Another major style phenomenon is “business casual” look. It implies comfortable clothes that are ideal to wear at the office.

WHO FOUNDED DRESS?
The World's First Dress? Known as the Tarkhan Dress, this rather elegant piece was found in a 5,000 year old Egyptian tomb. It was likely to have been the ancient Egyptian version of haute couture for its wealthy wearer. It nearly never came to light, as it was in a pile of rags that was overlooked for over 50 years
